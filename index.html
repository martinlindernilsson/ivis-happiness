Discovery process

I am interested in researching what makes people happy around the world. I plan on doing that by looking at an overall sense of happiness and how this relates to various factors such as state of health, trust, financial situation, religion and at how different countries differ when it comes to confidence in various institutions and organizations.
WVS provide data on percieved happiness To be able to show several parameters at once one would need to translate the data into a mean value of some sort, a decision has to be made about how to achieve that. One way is to bundle the top two answers and use this percentage as “happy”. Another way is to use some kind of grading system where “very happy” is worth more than “quite happy” and so on.
